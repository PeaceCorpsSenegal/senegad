Youth Clubs

SeneGAD currently supports Youth Clubs across Senegal. Youth Club activities vary depending on location, participation, and leadership. Every club is formed to reflect the specific needs of the community.

Some examples include:

Kedegou: a volunteer facilitated a club with 15-18 year old young girls where they discussed issues such as self-esteem, goal setting, female role models, HIV/AIDS, family planning, female health, and the importance of education for women.

Podor: PCVs organized a girls leadership conference with the MS Scholarship recipients and other village girls.

Most Girls' Club activities are initiated by PCVs and facilitated by host country nationals.
